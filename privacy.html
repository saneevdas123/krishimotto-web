Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026 · Version 1.0

KrishiMotto handles sensitive things: your identity, your land, your harvest and your bank details. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the control you have — including how to delete your account.

Please note: this document is a good-faith template written to reflect how the KrishiMotto platform actually works. It is not legal advice. Have qualified counsel review it against the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and your final partner arrangements before publishing.

1. Scope

This policy covers the KrishiMotto™ Android app and the Krishiaadhar platform (Admin, Warehouse and Lender portals) at krishiaadhar.gramtarang.org. It applies to everyone who uses the platform in any role — farmers, agri-experts, warehouse managers, lender admins, loan agents and platform admins. It should be read alongside our Terms & Conditions.

2. What we collect

Identity & contact

  • Your name, mobile number and the one-time passwords used to verify it; your preferred language.
  • For credit: e-KYC / identity information (including government identifiers such as Aadhaar-based verification where you complete it) and the verification result.

Land & farming data

  • Field boundaries you capture by GPS (tap-to-map), acreage, district/state, season and crop declarations.
  • Bookings and their context — crop type, preferred dates, purpose notes, photographs you upload.
  • Outputs generated for you: crop calendars, multispectral crop-health maps, hyperspectral soil reports, expert visit notes.

Storage, receipt & credit data

  • Storage requests, declared and weighed quantities, valuation, warehouse and bin allocation.
  • Digitised Receipts (e.g. KAC-2026-XXXXXX), Trust Deed QR identifiers and their verification status.
  • Credit applications: selected lender, requested amount and tenure, pledged collateral, bank account details for disbursement, and the lender's decision.

Payment data

  • Order references, amounts, status and timestamps. Payments are processed by our gateway partner (Instamoney) over QR/UPI — we do not store your full payment credentials, card data or UPI PIN.

Device, usage & AI interactions

  • Device and app version, coarse diagnostics, log data and IP address for security and debugging.
  • Questions you ask KrishiMotto AI and the responses given, so we can provide and improve advisory features.

Sensor data

  • Environmental telemetry from storage bays (temperature, humidity, pressure, gas readings, sensor and bin identifiers, and the device's location) associated with the produce you have stored.

3. Why we use it

  • To run your account — registration, OTP verification, language preference, support.
  • To deliver Services you book — routing a drone spray, expert visit or lab analysis to the right field and provider.
  • To take payment — creating and reconciling orders through the gateway.
  • To create and verify receipts — recording intake, generating the QR trust-deed and anchoring the receipt.
  • To facilitate credit — passing your application, KYC, bank and collateral evidence to the lender you choose so they can decide.
  • To prove collateral — showing live storage conditions against a receipt.
  • To keep the platform safe — preventing fraud such as double-pledging, forged trust deeds or impersonation.
  • To meet legal obligations — KYC/AML, tax, audit and lawful requests.
  • To improve the product — aggregated or de-identified analytics.

We rely on your consent (which you may withdraw), on the necessity of processing to perform the Services you request, and on our legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Who we share it with

  • Warehouse Partners — your name, contact, booking, declared crop and quantity, so they can accept and store your produce.
  • Lending Partners (and their loan agents) — when you pledge a receipt: your KYC and verification status, bank details for disbursement, the receipt, its collateral status and its telemetry.
  • Agri-Experts and service providers — the field, crop and booking context needed to perform the visit or analysis.
  • Payment gateway (Instamoney) — the order details required to collect payment.
  • e-KYC and infrastructure providers — identity verification, hosting, storage, logging and analytics, under contract and only for these purposes.
  • Public blockchain — see section 5.
  • Authorities — where required by law, court order, or to investigate fraud affecting collateral.
  • Successors — in a merger, acquisition or restructuring, subject to this policy.

5. Blockchain & public data

Receipts, collateral pledges, KYC attestations and disbursement events are anchored to an EVM-compatible chain (currently the Sepolia test network). We anchor identifiers, statuses and cryptographic hashes — not your name, Aadhaar number or bank details.

This is important: anything written to a public blockchain is public, permanent and cannot be deleted or amended — not by you, and not by us. That is what makes a receipt tamper-evident, and it is also a genuine limit on the right to erasure. Similarly, a Trust Deed QR resolves to a public verification page for the receipt; anyone holding the physical bag can scan it and see the receipt's status.

6. IoT telemetry

Where storage bays are instrumented, sensors report readings to our backend over a webhook. Telemetry is about storage conditions, not about you personally, but it is linked to your receipt and is visible to you, the Warehouse Partner, any lender holding the pledge, and platform admins. Crop Tracker is opt-in per harvest.

7. Retention

We keep personal data only as long as we need it:

  • Account data — while your account is active, then erased on deletion (subject to the exceptions below).
  • Transaction, receipt and storage records — retained for the statutory accounting and audit period, typically up to 8 years.
  • KYC and credit records — retained for the period required of us and of the Lending Partner under applicable KYC/AML rules.
  • Logs and diagnostics — typically up to 12 months.
  • Anchored blockchain records — permanent, by design.

8. Security

We use role-scoped API access, OTP-based authentication, encryption in transit, access controls and audit logging, and we contractually bind our processors. Sensitive credentials are held by the specialist providers (payments, e-KYC) rather than by us. No system is perfectly secure; please protect your device, never share an OTP, and tell us at once if you suspect misuse of your account.

9. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct data that is inaccurate or out of date;
  • Erase your data and close your account (section 10);
  • Withdraw consent for processing that relies on it — this may make parts of the platform unusable;
  • Nominate another person to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity;
  • Complain to us, and then to the relevant data-protection authority.

Write to privacy@krishimotto.app. We will verify your identity before acting and will respond within the period required by law.

10. Deleting your account

You can request deletion at any time from the Delete Account page, or by writing to privacy@krishimotto.app.

Once verified, we permanently erase your profile, mapped land boundaries, bookings, advisory outputs, AI conversations and receipt records held in our systems, normally within 30 days.

Some things survive deletion, and you should know this before you ask:

  • Deletion can be blocked or deferred while produce of yours is still in storage, a receipt is pledged as live collateral, or a credit facility is outstanding — those obligations must be settled first.
  • Records we must keep for tax, accounting, audit, KYC/AML or to defend a legal claim are retained for the statutory period, then erased.
  • Data already lawfully shared with a Lending Partner or Warehouse Partner is retained under their own policies and obligations — please contact them directly.
  • Blockchain-anchored records cannot be erased. They contain identifiers and hashes rather than your personal details.

11. Children

The platform is for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us and we will delete the account.

12. Changes

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Material changes will be notified in the app or by other reasonable means, and the "last updated" date above will change.
