When you map a field, you are asserting that you have the right to farm that land. Where a Service is fulfilled by a third party (an Agri-Expert, drone operator or laboratory), we facilitate the booking; the underlying work is performed by that provider using their own skill and judgement. Scheduled dates are targets and may shift for weather, safety, regulatory or operational reasons. Outputs such as crop calendars, spectral maps and soil reports are advisory — see clause 10.

6. Crop storage & warehouse receipts

When you request Crop Storage, you are booking space with a Warehouse Partner. On intake, the Warehouse Partner records the declared and actual weighed quantity, and the platform generates a Digitised Receipt and a Trust Deed QR that is affixed to the stored lot.

You confirm that produce you place into storage is lawfully yours, is free of any existing charge, lien or pledge, and is not subject to any dispute. The Digitised Receipt reflects what was weighed in; if the weighed quantity differs from your declaration, the weighed quantity prevails.

Physical custody, condition, insurance and release of the stored produce are the responsibility of the Warehouse Partner under their arrangement with you and with us. Once a receipt has been pledged as collateral, the associated stock may not be released to you until the pledge has been discharged.

Status of the receipt. A Digitised Receipt is a record generated by this platform. It is not a negotiable warehouse receipt issued under the Warehousing (Development and Regulation) Act, 2007, and it is not a security or financial instrument, unless and until we say otherwise in writing for a specific programme. Its function is to give warehouse operators and lenders verifiable evidence about stored produce.

7. Blockchain records

Receipts, collateral pledges, KYC attestations and disbursement events are anchored to an EVM-compatible blockchain, currently the Sepolia test network, in a gasless simulation mode. Each anchored event carries a transaction hash that can be independently inspected.

You should understand that:

  • Data written to a public chain is public, permanent and cannot be edited or deleted by us or by you. We anchor identifiers and cryptographic hashes rather than your personal details.
  • Test-network records carry no monetary value and provide no financial guarantee.
  • Blockchain networks are operated by third parties. We are not responsible for network outages, forks, reorganisations or changes in network rules.
  • We may migrate anchoring to a different network. Where we do, historic hashes remain as they were recorded.

8. IoT monitoring

Storage bays may be fitted with environmental sensors (for example a BME680 device reporting temperature, humidity, pressure and gas readings) that report to the platform over a webhook. Where you opt in, these readings appear in Crop Tracker and alongside the relevant receipt.

Telemetry is provided for information. Sensors can fail, lose connectivity or drift; readings and forecasts are indicative and are not a warranty of storage conditions, nor a substitute for the Warehouse Partner's own obligations regarding the produce.

9. Credit facilitation (PPACS)

We are not a lender. KrishiMotto facilitates an application: it lets you pledge a Digitised Receipt as collateral and routes the application, with the supporting KYC, bank and collateral evidence, to a Lending Partner you select.

The decision to approve, disburse, vary or reject any facility rests solely with the Lending Partner and its loan agents, who independently verify your identity, bank details and the pledged collateral. No application is guaranteed to be approved, and nothing in the app constitutes an offer of credit. Interest, tenure, charges, security and recovery are governed by the loan agreement between you and the Lending Partner, not by these Terms.

By pledging a receipt you authorise us to share the receipt, its telemetry, its verification status and your submitted KYC and bank information with the selected Lending Partner, and you agree that the pledged stock may be restricted from release until the pledge is discharged.
